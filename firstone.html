< First One – Tim Saltuklaroglu

This was my first official freeform doodle. When I began, I wasn’t sure how ‘free’ it should be, so when patterns emerged, I sometimes tried to shape them into recognizable forms. If you see a figure riding a horse or a dragon, with a bird overhead and a tethered soldier below, that may not be entirely accidental. Eventually, I realized that forcing patterns wasn’t my strength, and I began allowing each piece to evolve more naturally.
